Candlestick Anatomy & Formation

HIGH

Highest Price Reached

The maximum price level traded during the entire session - represents the peak of buying pressure before sellers stepped in

CLOSE

Final Settlement Price

The last price traded when the session ended - shows who won the battle between buyers and sellers

OPEN

Session Starting Price

The first price traded when the session began - sets the baseline for measuring the session's price movement

LOW

Lowest Price Reached

The minimum price level traded during the entire session - represents peak selling pressure before buyers defended

BODY

Open-to-Close Range

The thick rectangle showing the distance between open and close - larger bodies indicate stronger conviction and momentum

WICKS

High/Low Extensions

Thin lines extending from the body to session highs/lows - reveal price levels that were tested but rejected by the market
